# Gadag Hackathon Project – Student Dropout Prediction System

## Overview

I participated in the **Gadag Hackathon**, where our team developed a solution to address the problem of **student dropouts in educational institutions**.
The goal of the project was to use **Machine Learning** to identify students who are at risk of dropping out and provide early intervention strategies.

Student dropout is a serious challenge for schools and colleges because it affects academic success, institutional performance, and student careers. Our system helps institutions detect at-risk students early and take corrective actions.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students discontinue their education due to various reasons such as:

* Poor academic performance
* Low attendance
* Financial difficulties
* Lack of engagement
* Personal or social challenges

Educational institutions often detect these issues **too late**, when the student has already decided to leave.
Therefore, a system is required to **predict dropout risk in advance**.

---

## Proposed Solution

We developed a **Student Dropout Risk Prediction System** that analyzes student data and predicts the likelihood of a student dropping out.

The system uses **machine learning algorithms** to analyze patterns in student behavior and academic records.

### Key Idea

Predict the **risk level of each student** so teachers and administrators can provide timely support.

---

## Machine Learning Model

We used a **Random Forest Classifier** to predict the dropout risk of students.

### Input Features

Some of the important parameters used in the model include:

* Attendance percentage
* Previous academic performance
* Study hours
* Participation in activities
* Assignment completion rate
* Socio-economic factors

### Output

The system predicts the student's risk level:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk
